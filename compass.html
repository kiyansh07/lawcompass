About LawCompass

LawCompass is a mentorship initiative started by students of BHU pursuing B.A. LL.B. Our objective is simple — to help sincere aspirants navigate entrance exams, college choices, internships, and initial exposure to law with clarity and discipline.

We do not promise shortcuts. We offer direction, structure, and honest feedback.
